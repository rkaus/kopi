About

I source green beans from places and people who share my love of coffee, then I roast them and share them with people who like good coffee. Looking forward to sharing the next cup with you!

If you would like to try some coffee, please fill out the contact form below. Roasts are typically medium to medium-dark (light city to full city) based on the source and profile of the beans.
